About Me

From Frontend to UI/UX: A Natural Transition

Hi again! I'm Huỳnh Ngọc Bảo Châu - a fresh graduate from University of Information Technology - VNU HCM, majoring in Computer Networks and Data Communications.

My path into UI/UX wasn't a straight line. It started with frontend development. During my 4-month internship at VNPT-IT2 as an Angular developer, I worked with mentors to turn design specs into actual interfaces. I spent hours debugging, optimizing, and making sure every pixel matched the mockups. I loved that precision, but I kept wondering: what's the story behind those designs? Why were they made that way?

That curiosity led me to my first real UI/UX group project: YumDaily, a social culinary network. My teammates and I did user surveys, built wireframes, designed 15+ screens, and tested prototypes. For the first time, I wasn't just implementing someone else's vision, I was solving real problems for real people.

Now, I'm fully focused on UI/UX design. With my background in frontend, I naturally think about how designs come to life in real products, so I care a lot about practicality, consistency, and the small details that make things feel just right.

I’m still learning and growing, but I enjoy every step of the process, especially when a simple idea turns into something people can actually use and enjoy.
